About This Map

This live weather radar is sourced from Environment Canada via their GeoMet API. The radar updates every 10 minutes, and warnings are refreshed at the same interval.

Understanding the Data:

  • The radar layer shows precipitation intensity in dBZ. Higher values indicate heavier precipitation.
  • The alerts layer highlights active weather warnings, watches, and advisories.
  • The red markers indicate locations of issued warnings. Click them for details.
