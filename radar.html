About This Map: This live weather radar is sourced from Environment Canada via their GeoMet API. The radar updates every 10 minutes, and warnings are refreshed at the same interval.

Understanding the Data: - The **radar layer** shows precipitation intensity in dBZ. Higher values indicate heavier precipitation. - The **alerts layer** highlights active weather warnings, watches, and advisories. - The **red markers** indicate locations of issued warnings. Click them for details.
Disclaimer: This map is for informational purposes only. Radar and warning updates may be delayed, and users should verify information through official sources such as Environment Canada or local authorities.
